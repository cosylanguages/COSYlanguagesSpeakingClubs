Let's Celebrate

National Financial Awareness Day

Vocabulary

  • Financial: Relating to finance or money.
  • Awareness: Knowledge or perception of a situation or fact.
  • Donation: Something that is given to a charity, especially a sum of money.
  • Wellness: The state of being in good health, especially as an actively pursued goal.
  • Independence: The fact or state of being independent.
  • Recovery: A return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength.
  • Anniversary: The date on which an event took place in a previous year.
  • Featured: Having or including a prominent part or role.
  • Promotions: Activities to increase the sales of a product or service.
  • Calendar: A chart or series of pages showing the days, weeks, and months of a particular year.

Round One: Yes/No Questions

  1. Is this holiday about spending money?
  2. Is financial awareness important for everyone?
  3. Is this a federal holiday?
  4. Does the website mention any deals for this day?
  5. Is this holiday celebrated in August?
  6. Is the goal of this day to make people feel bad about their finances?
  7. Are there any other holidays mentioned for the same day?
  8. Is this holiday related to a specific historical event?
  9. Does the website provide tips for financial wellness?
  10. Is this a new holiday?

  4. This holiday is just a marketing gimmick.
